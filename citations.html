Supreme Court Analytics & Visualizations



Outgoing (black) arrows represent cases that the given (center) case cites, and incoming (green) arrows represent cases that cite the given case. To start, we have arbitrarily chosen Gomez v. United States District Court (1992). Click (and drag) circles to redraw the graph based on another connected case, or search for a new one above.



The data for these graphs comes from decisions on FindLaw. Click on the text of any case to see the case on FindLaw, if available. The dataset contains most cases after 1900.
